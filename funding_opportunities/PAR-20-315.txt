section viii. other informationthe lasker clinical research scholars (lasker scholars) program honors the contributions of mary and albert lasker to the nih and to the overall biomedical community, by addressing the clinical research mission of the nih: better treatments for disease leading to improvements in human health (par-16-203). this limited competition foa offers an opportunity for current lasker scholars (si2 awardees) to continue developing their independent careers at an extramural research institution, by applying for up to 5 years of extramural r00 grant funding.r00 transition award: lasker scholars (current si2 awardees) awarded under rfa-od-11-001, or rfa-od-12-001, or rfa-od-13-004, or rfa-od-14-004, who have been offered, and have accepted, a full-time tenure-track or tenured position at an extramural sponsoring institution/organization, are able to receive up to 5 years of r00 support to continue their research as independent clinician scientists. scholars can submit their application in their fourth or later year of the program, and need not have an offer from an extramural sponsoring institution/organization at the time of submission. by the time of r00 award, scholars must have successfully completed at least five years in the intramural phase of the program. scholars who choose to leave the lasker program before completing 5 years of research in the nih intramural research program will not be eligible for the lasker scholars transition award (r00) but will be eligible to compete for other extramural nih research grants as announced in the nih guide for grants and contracts.note: lasker scholars (current si2 awardees) awarded under par-15-189, or par-16-203, and more recent foas, should follow the instructions provided under each of those
